Evolution of Phones

  • 1990s: Basic mobile phones for calls and texts.
  • 2007: Apple launched the first iPhone with a touchscreen interface.
  • 2010s: Smartphones began replacing cameras, computers, and GPS devices.
  • 2020s: Foldable phones, AI features, and 5G became standard in flagships.
