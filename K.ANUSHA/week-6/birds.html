Fun Facts About Birds

  • The smallest bird is the bee hummingbird – just 5.5 cm long!
  • Ostriches can't fly, but they can run up to 70 km/h.
  • Some parrots can mimic human speech.
  • Peregrine falcons dive at speeds over 300 km/h – the fastest animals on Earth.
  • Birds have hollow bones to help them fly.
