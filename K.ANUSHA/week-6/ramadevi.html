About Ramadevi

Ramadevi Choudhury was an Indian freedom fighter and social reformer from Odisha. She dedicated her life to the service of the poor, the upliftment of women, and the cause of independence. Inspired by Mahatma Gandhi, she actively participated in the non-violent movement and worked tirelessly for women's education and rights.

Timeline

1899

Born in Cuttack, Odisha

1921

Joined the Non-Cooperation Movement with Mahatma Gandhi

1930

Arrested for participating in Salt Satyagraha

1950s

Established schools and women’s welfare centers across Odisha

1985

Passed away, leaving behind a legacy of social change
